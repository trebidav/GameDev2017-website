As Blind As A Cat

A 2017 MFF UK GameDev Game

The Cat is lost.
Far from home.
Is there someone to help her out?

See the alpha version gameplay:



'As Blind as a Cat', a side view puzzle platformer, is a story of an old kitty that strolled too far from the safety of its home and now, as the night has fallen, found itself completely lost in the darkness. Player will assume the role of a bat who must navigate this almost blind kitty through various dangers of the night and help it get back home to safety. Using various objects – cat food, jingle bell and more – the bat will tell the kitty where to go, where to jump, or where to duck and crawl. You as the bat need to carefully plan how and when use those precious resources in order to guide your scared cat companion into the light.
